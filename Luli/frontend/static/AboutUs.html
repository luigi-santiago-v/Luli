Luli Hydroponics was born out of a common interest between friends, peers, and vegans. Our goal is to bring the means of production to your table top (at retail price) with our automated hydroponic system. We are passionate about growing healthy greens and automating the process. Each new user is one more urban farmer, weekend harvester, and die hard horticulturalist.
